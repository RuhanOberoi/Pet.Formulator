Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3 May 2026 · Last Updated: 3 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PetForm Pro ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares your personal information when you use our website and services at petfoodformulator.com (the "Service").

PetForm Pro is operated as a sole trader business based in Australia. We comply with the Australian Privacy Principles under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA) for California residents.

Quick Summary: We collect what we need to run the Service (your email, formulas you create, payment info processed by Stripe). We don't sell your data. You can export or delete your account at any time. Contact: admin.petfoodformulator@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Email address and password when you register. We use Supabase for authentication; passwords are stored as cryptographic hashes and we never see your plain-text password.
  • Pet and recipe data: Information about your pet (species, life stage, weight) and any recipes, ingredients, or regulatory standards you create or import.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to a paid plan, payment details are collected and processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not see, store, or have access to your full credit card number. We receive only a customer ID, billing email, and subscription status.
  • Communications: Any messages you send us via email or support channels.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: How you interact with the Service (pages visited, features used, AI requests made). This helps us improve the product and enforce usage limits.
  • Technical data: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and timestamps. Stored briefly for security and debugging.
  • AI usage records: The number of AI requests you make, the model used, and approximate token counts. Used to enforce plan limits and detect abuse.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect biometric data, location data, or contact lists.
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use this Service.
  • We do not use third-party advertising trackers or behavioural ad cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service: Authenticate your account, save your formulas, run nutritional calculations, generate AI responses.
  • To process payments: Manage subscriptions, process refunds, send billing receipts.
  • To improve the Service: Understand which features are used, fix bugs, plan new features.
  • To communicate with you: Send transactional emails (account verification, password resets, billing receipts), respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Tax records, fraud prevention, responding to lawful requests from authorities.

We will not sell your personal information to third parties. We will not use your formulas or recipes to train AI models without your explicit consent.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK Users)

If you are in the EU, EEA, or UK, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract: To provide the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve the Service, prevent fraud, and ensure security.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with tax, accounting, and regulatory requirements.
  • Consent: For optional features (e.g., marketing emails, if any). You can withdraw consent at any time.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and local storage to operate the Service:

  • Essential cookies: For authentication (keeping you logged in) and saving your preferences. These cannot be disabled without breaking the Service.
  • Local storage: Used to cache your formulas locally for offline access and faster load times.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or behavioural tracking. We do not currently use analytics platforms like Google Analytics. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this policy and ask for consent where required.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Account data, formulas, and ingredients are deleted within 30 days.
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years to comply with Australian tax law.
  • Backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before being permanently overwritten.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 All Users

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export: Download your formulas, ingredients, and other data in CSV or JSON format from within the app.

7.2 GDPR / UK Users (Additional Rights)

  • Restriction of processing: Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint: With your local data protection authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, your national DPA in the EU).

7.3 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know: What personal information we collect, use, disclose.
  • Right to delete: Subject to certain exceptions (e.g., billing records).
  • Right to correct: Inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt-out of sale: We do not sell your information, so this right is automatically respected.
  • Right to non-discrimination: We will not deny service or charge different prices for exercising your rights.

7.4 Australian Users (Privacy Act 1988)

  • Rights under the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), including the right to access and correct your personal information.
  • You may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

To exercise any of these rights, email admin.petfoodformulator@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing requests.

8. Data Security

We take security seriously and use industry-standard practices:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Passwords are stored using one-way cryptographic hashing (bcrypt via Supabase Auth).
  • Database access is protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies — users can only access their own data.
  • API keys for third-party services are stored as encrypted environment variables, never exposed to browsers.
  • Backups are encrypted at rest by our infrastructure providers.

However, no system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law.

9. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email or through the Service. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
